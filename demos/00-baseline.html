The Current Background

This is your baseline: a simple, flat #013250 background color. It's clean and straightforward, but there's opportunity to add subtle sophistication.

What We're Starting From

The deep ocean blue (#013250) provides excellent contrast with your cream text (#dcd9d6) and coral links (#f57a59). It's readable, professional, and works well.

However, flat backgrounds can feel a bit... flat. Modern web design often incorporates subtle texture, depth, or movement to add visual interest without compromising readability.

The Goal

We want to enhance this background in ways that are:

  • Barely perceptible at first glance
  • Sophisticated and professional
  • Inspired by your astronomical aesthetic (Milky Way, aurora)
  • Performance-conscious
  • Complementary to your content, not distracting

Think of it like adding a subtle film grain to a photograph—you might not notice it consciously, but it adds richness and depth that makes the image feel more premium.

Compare The Techniques

Open multiple demo tabs side-by-side to see the differences. Some effects are deliberately subtle—you may need to look closely or switch between tabs to notice them. That's the point.

The most successful background enhancements are the ones you feel rather than see.
